Published: 2026-04-23 00:52 | Score: 92

SOON, an AI-driven full-stack game development platform by Jiyi AI, has launched its second round of testing. The platform features a 'video-to-game' capability that uses multimodal models to translate gameplay footage into functional code and logic. It also offers automated character rigging and animation, allowing developers to export assets directly to engines like Unity. Aimed at the fast-paced mini-game market, SOON significantly reduces the cost of prototype validation and commercial-grade production.
